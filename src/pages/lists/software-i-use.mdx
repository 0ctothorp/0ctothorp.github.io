# Software I use

Software that is worth mentioning.

## Uncategorized

- [mullvad vpn](https://mullvad.net/) - privacy oriented VPN
- [scrcpy](https://scrcpy.org/) - control your android phone on your PC
- [yt-dlp](https://github.com/yt-dlp/yt-dlp) - video downloader for youtube and many other services

## File sharing

- [syncthing](https://syncthing.net/) - synchronize (backup) files automatically between your devices through your local network.
- [LocalSend](https://localsend.org/) - send files between your devices without using cloud
    - I haven't tried it yet, but am interested in checking it out.

## Browser extensions

- [SponsorBlock](https://sponsor.ajay.app/) - fantastic crowd sourced extension that let's you easily skip sponsored segments in youtube videos
